The rise of AI agents is creating a new “intention economy,” where autonomous systems act on user goals rather than just capturing attention. This shift, driven by generative AI and decentralized technologies, is set to automate complex tasks, from financial transactions to travel planning. As these agents become more capable, they are not only transforming user experiences but also reshaping economic models by enabling outcome-based pricing and creating new markets for data, security, and infrastructure. While this transition presents challenges in trust and governance, it ultimately promises a more efficient, equitable, and user-centric web.
